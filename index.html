About Easy Gym

I created Easy Gym for my personal training and decided to share it with friends and with anyone who enjoys exercising. If you would like additional functionality, please contact me at stivenson@stivenson.com.

Plan

Choose Mon or Tue or Wed etc. Create and save a day plan.

Do-it

Choose your saved training day, press Start Training, complete sets, and save the training.

Progress

Saved training is grouped by date, training time, exercise, sets, kg, and reps.

Data

Backup exports all app data as a JSON file. Restore imports it back. Reset becomes available after a backup.
